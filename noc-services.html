NOC Services

A Network Operations Center (NOC) is a centralized team or facility responsible for monitoring, managing, and maintaining the health and performance of networking devices and services. NOC services ensure high availability, minimize downtime, and proactively resolve issues before they impact business operations NOC Services provide 24×7 monitoring, management, and support for an organization's IT and network infrastructure. The primary goal is to ensure maximum network availability, performance, and security.

NOC-Basic

  • Monitor your networking devices
  • 24x7 monitoring
  • Automated email alerts

NOC-Plus

  • Monitor your networking devices
  • 24x7 monitoring
  • Automated email alerts
  • Level 2 Support
  • RCA Reporting
  • Incident Managment
  • Monitoring Network Performance
  • Monthly reporting
